About Kazan

Kazan: The Meeting Point of Culture Kazan is the capital and largest city of the Republic of Tatarstan, Russia. It is often called the "Third Capital of Russia" because of its historic al and economic importance. The city is famous for being a place where Europe and Asia meet, as it beautifully blends Muslim and Christian cultures.

History

The most famous landmark is the Kazan Kremlin, a UNESCO World Heritage site that features b oth the magnificent Kul Sharif Mosque and the Annunciation Cathedral.
